Excel data of GZ are all offered by a relevant department of Yamanashi Prefecture.

These excel data are combined into a dataset as "GZlist_raw.csv".

"GZlist.csv" is the dataset made from "GZlist_raw.csv" via the website of Center for Spatial Information Science of the University of Tokyo (https://geocode.csis.u-tokyo.ac.jp/home/csv-admatch/).
